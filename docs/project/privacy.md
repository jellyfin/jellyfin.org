# GDPR Privacy Policy for Jellyfin (https://jellyfin.org)

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data controller

   Jellyfin is the data controller. You can contact us at team@jellyfin.org.

2. Personal data we collect

   • IP addresses

3. Purposes of processing

   • to provide and maintain our Service

4. Legal basis

   • Our legitimate interests

5. Data sharing

   We do not share your data with third parties, except as required by law.

6. International transfers

   We may transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

   Logs containing IP addresses will be moved to servers outside the EEA to ensure secure storage and functionality of automated security systems. Data at rest is protected against 3rd party access via industry standard encryption and is never shared with any 3rd party for any reason other than to ensure the secure operation of Jellyfin's services or other reasons as explicitly declared in this policy.

7. Data retention

   Logs are stored for 7 days and are automatically deleted after that time period.

8. Security measures

   • SSL/TLS encryption

   • Regular security audits

9. Cookies

   We do not use cookies.

10. Your rights

    You have the following rights under the GDPR:

    • right to rectification

    • right to erasure

    To exercise these rights, contact us at team@jellyfin.org.

11. Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

    If you have questions about this policy, contact us at team@jellyfin.org.

    https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement
