# Privacy Policy

The Jellyfin project strives not to collect personal data from its users. In the course of normal operations, however, some private data may be collected for technical reasons.

This policy describes what data is collected and which systems or services it applies to.

## General

- We do not collect any data beyond what is described below.
- We do not share or transfer data to any third parties except as required by law.

## Jellyfin Server

- The Jellyfin server installed on your own computer contains no telemetry functionality. No data except that explicitly described below is transmitted to the Jellyfin project.
- As a scheduled task, the server contacts our official plugin repository at `https://repo.jellyfin.org`, hosted on [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com), to obtain plugin manifests, cover images, and plugin binaries for installation. See the next section for details.

## Jellyfin Clients

- Individual Jellyfin clients may collect platform-specific data. Each client has its own privacy policy.

## Jellyfin Self-Hosted Services

- Jellyfin provides several services hosted by the project itself on [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com) to support development and operations:
  - Repository and mirror system (`https://repo.jellyfin.org`)
  - Support forum (`https://forum.jellyfin.org`)
  - Weblate translation engine (`https://translate.jellyfin.org`)
  - Fider feature request tracker (`https://features.jellyfin.org`)
  - Demonstration instances (`https://demo.jellyfin.org`)
- Requests to access these services include your public IP address and requested URL.
- This information is logged and stored for technical evaluation for no more than seven days.
- This information is stored on systems under the jurisdiction of Ontario, Canada, and for localized geodiverse mirror instances, the jurisdiction of the local mirror system based on GeoIP redirection.
- Services that require registration, such as the forum, Weblate, and Fider, also store:
  - Usernames
  - Email addresses
  - Any additional information you choose to provide in your profile
- Data provided to these services is retained indefinitely unless removal is explicitly requested.
- To remove personally identifiable data, use the Forum Data Erasure functionality in the user control panel or contact [our core team](https://jellyfin.org/contact).
- Submitted posts and content are retained and anonymized after a data erasure request. We do not delete publicly submitted posts except as required by law or in accordance with our moderation policies.

## Other Services

- Jellyfin may use other services covered by their own privacy policies, such as OpenCollective for donations or GitHub for code development. See the relevant provider policies for details.

## GDPR Compliance for the Jellyfin website (https://jellyfin.org)

<!-- markdownlint-enable MD034 -->

- Data controller: Jellyfin. Contact us at `team@jellyfin.org`.
- Personal data we collect: IP addresses.
- Purpose of processing: to provide and maintain our service.
- Legal basis: our legitimate interests.
- Data sharing: we do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.
- International transfers: we may transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Logs containing IP addresses may be stored outside the EEA to support secure storage and automated security systems. Data at rest is protected against third-party access via industry-standard encryption and is not shared except to ensure the secure operation of Jellyfin's services or as otherwise described in this policy.
- Data retention: logs are stored for seven days and are automatically deleted after that time.
- Security measures:
  - SSL/TLS encryption
  - Regular security audits
- Cookies: we do not use cookies.
- Your rights:
  - Right to rectification
  - Right to erasure
  - To exercise these rights, contact us at `team@jellyfin.org`.
- Changes to this policy: we may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
- Contact: if you have questions about this policy, contact us at `team@jellyfin.org`.
