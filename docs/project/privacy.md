# Privacy Policy

The Jellyfin project strives not to collect personal data from its users. However, in the course of normal operations, some private data may be collected for technical reasons.

This policy seeks to outline the precise nature of such data and which systems and/or services they apply to.

## General

* We will not collect any data outside of that described below.

* We will not share or transfer any data to any third parties for any reason except as required by law.

## Jellyfin Server

* The Jellyfin server system itself, installed on your own computer, contains no telemetry functionality. No data except that explicitly declared below is transmitted to the Jellyfin project.

* The Jellyfin server system will, as a scheduled task, reach out to our official Plugin repository system at `https://repo.jellyfin.org`, hosted via [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com), to obtain a manifest of plugins and cover images for those plugins, as well as to obtain plugin binary files for installation. See the following section for details.

## Jellyfin Clients

* Individual Jellyfin clients may collect their own data based on the platform. Each client will have its own privacy policy which governs that specific client.

## Jellyfin Self-Hosted Services

* Jellyfin provides several services hosted by the project itself via [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com) to aid in the development and support of the project. These include:

   * Repository & Mirror System (`https://repo.jellyfin.org`)

   * Support Forum (`https://forum.jellyfin.org`)

   * Weblate Translation Engine (`https://translate.jellyfin.org`)

   * Fider Feature Request Tracker (`https://features.jellyfin.org`)

   * Demonstration instances (`https://demo.jellyfin.org`)

* When making requests to access these services:

   * These requests will contain your **public** IP address and URL.

   * This information will be logged and stored for technical evaluation for a period of not more than seven (7) days.

   * This information will be stored on systems under the jurisdiction of Ontario, Canada, and for localized geodiverse mirror instances, the jurisdiction of the local mirror system (one of: the United States of America; The United Kingdom of Great Britan and Northern Ireland; The European Union; India; Singapore; or Australia) based on geoIP redirection.

* In addition, services which require registration (Forum, Weblate, Fider) will also store:

   * Usernames

   * Email Addresses

   * Any additional information you choose to provide in your Profile on the service

* Data provided to the aforementioned services will be retained indefinitely unless removal is explicitly requested.

* Should you wish to remove personally-identifyable data, please leverage the Forum Data Erasure functionality ("User control panel" -> "Personal Data Erasure") or contact [our core team](https://jellyfin.org/contact). See section 10 below for additional details.

* Submitted posts/content will be retained and anonymized after completion of a Data Erasure request. We will not delete publicly-submitted posts except as required by law or in accordance with our moderation policies.

## Other

Jellyfin may leverage other services which are covered by their own Privacy Policy, e.g. OpenCollective for donations or GitHub for code development. For more information on policies specific to those platforms, please see their respective Privacy Policy documents.

For any other questions or concerns, please reach out to [our core team](https://jellyfin.org/contact).

# GDPR Policy for Jellyfin (https://jellyfin.org)

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data controller

   Jellyfin is the data controller. You can contact us at team@jellyfin.org.

2. Personal data we collect

   • IP addresses

3. Purposes of processing

   • to provide and maintain our Service

4. Legal basis

   • Our legitimate interests

5. Data sharing

   We do not share your data with third parties, except as required by law.

6. International transfers

   We may transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

   Logs containing IP addresses will be moved to servers outside the EEA to ensure secure storage and functionality of automated security systems. Data at rest is protected against 3rd party access via industry standard encryption and is never shared with any 3rd party for any reason other than to ensure the secure operation of Jellyfin's services or other reasons as explicitly declared in this policy.

7. Data retention

   Logs are stored for 7 days and are automatically deleted after that time period.

8. Security measures

   • SSL/TLS encryption

   • Regular security audits

9. Cookies

   We do not use cookies.

10. Your rights

    You have the following rights under the GDPR:

    • right to rectification

    • right to erasure

    To exercise these rights, contact us at team@jellyfin.org.

11. Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

    If you have questions about this policy, contact us at team@jellyfin.org.
